About Us

Vision, Mission, Values


OBJECTIVE (VISION)
To be a leading non-governmental organization that realizes the sustainability of forest ecosystem services in order to leave a livable world to future generations, is the subject of achieving global development goals and a carbon neutral world, guides the society to protect natural assets and the planet, and leaves meaningful traces on the road to a green future.

MISSION (MISSION)
To be the pioneer of green transformation with national and international collaborations by using science-based activities, forestation activities and inclusive education power in the focus of our values ​​for the protection and enhancement of natural assets.

OUR VALUES
We are constantly improving: We act according to science, work with determination to reach our goals, and improve ourselves by raising the bar every year. We are impartial and consistent: We carry out our work independently of politics, impartially, in unity of action and discourse, and in the focus of our values. We are transparent and accountable: While ensuring the integrity of our activities in an open and transparent manner, we respect the privacy rights of all our supporters and act with a sense of mutual trust and responsibility. We are stronger with our cooperation: We believe in the power of our local, national and international cooperation, and we continue to work for a sustainable world by coming together with institutions and organizations that are experts in their fields.
